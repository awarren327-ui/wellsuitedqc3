Our Story

Founded on lived experience.
Fueled by community.

Embrace Race · Davenport
Tracy Singleton — Founder, in her own words

Tracy Singleton didn't build Well Suited in a boardroom. She built it from the memory of her own kitchen table — from the days when she was the mother, wondering how the gas bill would get paid or where the next meal would come from. Tracy knows what it's like to fight for stability, and that lived experience is the heartbeat of every choice she makes.

Today, as the founder of Well Suited and Executive Director of the TMBC Lincoln Center, Tracy has created a lifeline in Davenport. She's building an ecosystem that doesn't just see where underserved youth are, but illuminates exactly how far they can go.

We pair our young people with mentors and leaders from across the Quad Cities. We aren't here to lecture; we are here to walk beside them. Our mission is simple: to show every student what is possible when someone truly believes in their potential.

Why It Matters

A single mentor relationship
can change a life.

55%more likely to enroll in college
than peers without mentors
Source: MENTOR National — The Mentoring Effect Report

The numbers behind mentorship are clear.

Youth with consistent mentors are significantly more likely to graduate, attend college, hold leadership positions, and avoid risk behaviors. But statistics only tell part of the story.

What we see at every Well Suited event is something the data can't capture — the moment a young person stands a little taller because somebody showed up for them. The shift in their voice when they're asked about their dreams. The light that comes back on.

That's why this work matters. And that's why we need you.

Programs

Real experiences. Real relationships.
Real change.

Every program is designed around one idea: exposure changes everything. When a young person sees their future reflected in a mentor, the trajectory shifts.

The Tuxedo Experience

Mentors and youth suit up together — learning how to tie a tie, carry themselves with confidence, and present their best selves. The day ends with a professional photo shoot and a dinner with speakers, because looking sharp and feeling valued go hand in hand.

Signature Event

Embrace Race Experience

The flagship annual gathering brings 100+ young people together with CEOs, community leaders, and professionals for keynotes, "networking bingo," student panels, and real talk about overcoming obstacles and building a future worth pursuing.

Annual Gathering

Lincoln Center Hub

A permanent home base at the TMBC Lincoln Center in Davenport where young people can show up, get resources, receive college guidance, or simply have a safe space with adults who are genuinely invested in their growth.
